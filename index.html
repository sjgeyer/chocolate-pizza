For the fir-swirlL Melt butter over medium heat in a saucepan. Add brown sugar and stir to dissolve. Halve all of the figs and toss in the saucepan with water and lemon juice. Cook over medium heat, stirring frequently, until you have a chunky-jammy mixture. Add salt with one or two stirs., set aside and let cool completely.

Ice cream: In a small pot over medium heat, combine milk, and granulated sugar until sugar is completely dissolved and the milk is just barely lukewarm. Whisk in the egg yokes. Set mixturein the fridge and wait until the fig mixture is cooled.

Using an ice cream machine, pour liquids into the frozzen basin and process according to manufacturer instructions, i.e., let spinand thicken for 20 minutes beforeadding mascarpone, fig jam mixture, and the nuts. Continue to proccess for +/- 10 minutes. Pour semi-frozen mixture into a pyrex dish or glass tupperware. Freese for at least two hours before serving.

  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 1/3 granulated sugar
  • 2 egg yokes
  • 1 lemon
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup honey roasted pecans,()roughly chopped
